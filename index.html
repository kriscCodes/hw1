About Us

Bryan's Bakery is a family-owned bakery that has been serving the Fordham community since 2020. We are committed to providing the best service and food that feels like home. From our sandwiches to our cakes we hope you enjoy it all!

Every morning we wake up early to bake fresh breads, pastries, and all your favorite treats. We use only the best ingredients and make everything from scratch. Whether you need your morning coffee fix, a quick lunch, or something sweet for dessert, we've got you covered!
